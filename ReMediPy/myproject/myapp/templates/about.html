Welcome to Re-Medi

If thereâ€™s something that saw an uproar during the Covid-19 pandemic, it was 3 online marketplaces. However, online pharmacies remained to be a less common idea. Whereas, medicine was a critical need during the time of a pandemic that spread so quickly.


Even today, one cannot easily find an online drug store in Dhaka in the time of their need.


This observation motivated our team to attempt to make a social contribution by creating an online drug store website that will deliver all over Dhaka City at any given time.


ReMedi, derived from the word remedy, is our combined effort to create an epharmacy website that will ensure quick delivery 24/7 anywhere in Dhaka City via third-party delivery platforms, take medicine requests from users with proper prescription, let users search through the pharmacy inventory, deliver medicine from nearby pharmacies instead if the drug is not in our stock, and provide 5 medicine information, etc., using the website.
